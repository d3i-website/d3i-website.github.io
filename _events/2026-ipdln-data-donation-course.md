This half-day workshop at the IPDLN Conference introduces data donation as a privacy-preserving approach for collecting digital trace data. Participants work through the full workflow of a data donation study: study design, configuring the Port software, writing extraction scripts, recruiting participants, and linking donated data with other data sources.

The course takes place on July 13, 2026, 09:00–12:30, at Erasmus University Rotterdam, and is taught by Laura Boeschoten and Danielle McCool (Utrecht University). Registration runs through the [IPDLN conference registration](https://ipdln.org/registration/); see the [IPDLN 2026 program](https://virtual.oxfordabstracts.com/event/76116/program) for details.
