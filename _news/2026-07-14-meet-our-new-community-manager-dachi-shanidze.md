We are pleased to welcome Dachi Shanidze as the new community manager for the Data Donation Infrastructure (D3I) and TDCC RIGHTS projects.

Dachi is responsible for supporting the daily operations of the projects and assisting researchers with administrative tasks, including budget overviews, communication activities, and event planning.

Alongside his work with Data Donation Infrastructure, Dachi is a Programme Manager at the NWO-funded ELSA Lab AI for Health Equity and Office Manager at the Amsterdam School of Communication Research (ASCoR).

Before joining Data Donation Infrastructure, Dachi worked as a Programme Manager at the Amsterdam Centre for European Studies and as a Programme Assistant at the National Democratic Institute (NDI).

Dachi holds a Master’s degree in International Affairs from the University of Groningen.
