Port is being used by a growing number of researchers across Europe and beyond. Since its first deployment in 2022, adoption has accelerated each year — across institutions in five countries and three continents, and spanning a wide range of digital platforms. The figures below track that growth, where studies are run, and which platforms researchers have studied. In 2025, D3I received structural funding to support researchers at Dutch institutions with free access to the infrastructure.

## Help us track adoption

Port is funded through multiple grants, and for reporting to these funders
it is important that we keep statistics on usage. If you have used Port for
your study, we kindly request you to fill in a short questionnaire once
your data donation study goes live.
