## Data Donation Day 2022

Data Donation Day, held on 11 March 2022 at Utrecht University, brought together applied researchers, methodologists, research engineers and software developers with an interest in using digital trace data for scientific research. The event ran in hybrid format at the Boothzaal of the University Library in Utrecht and on Microsoft Teams.

The organizing committee was Laura Boeschoten, Daniel Oberski, Kyuri Park, Qixiang Fang, and Marianne Geelhoed.

For the original event page, including the full program, speakers, and abstracts, see the [Data Donation Day announcement on hds.sites.uu.nl](https://hds.sites.uu.nl/2022/01/15/data-donation-day/).
