## Data Donation Symposium 2023

The 2023 Data Donation Symposium took place on 11–12 September 2023 in Zurich, hosted by the University of Zurich. It offered a platform to discuss data donation as an approach to make digital trace data accessible for academic research.

The keynote, "Beyond data donation — some practical examples," was given by Paul-Olivier Dehaye. Five sessions covered empirical studies, legal aspects, bias in data donation studies, data donation collection tools, and methodological considerations.

For the full program, abstracts, and registration archive, see the [original event page on datadonation.uzh.ch](https://datadonation.uzh.ch/en/symposium-2023/).
