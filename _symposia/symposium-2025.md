## Data Donation Symposium 2025

After a succesful 2024 Data Donation Symposium in Amsterdam, our [colleagues in Germany](https://data-donation-symposium.ifkw.lmu.de/organizing-team/) hosted the 2025 Data Donation Symposium. The event took place on October 9 and 10 at the Bavarian Academy of Sciences and Humanities.
